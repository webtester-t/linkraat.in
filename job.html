                      About SkillMatch India

                      ×

                      SkillMatch India is a revolutionary job portal designed to bridge the gap between talented professionals and top employers across the country. Our platform focuses on skill-based matching, ensuring that the right candidate meets the right opportunity.

                      Founded in 2020, we've grown to serve over 10,000 companies and 500,000 job seekers. Our mission is to make hiring transparent, efficient, and merit-driven. We believe that skills define a person's potential, not just their designation.

                      Why choose us? Our advanced filtering system allows you to search by multiple skills, cities, states, and countries simultaneously — just like adding tags. We're committed to providing a seamless experience for both employers and job seekers.

                      Our Vision: To become India's most trusted skill-first hiring platform, empowering careers and businesses alike.
